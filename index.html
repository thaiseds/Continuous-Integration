Welcome to our FRIENDS Website!

Author: Thais Escridelli da Silveira, Published: 04/13/2022

Here you can find curious facts about this comedy that make us fall in love for all characters even so many years after the end of the TV Show.
The first episode launched on september, 1994 and ended on May, 2004. Even almost 20 years after the end of the TV show, they continue delighting publics of all ages.
In 2022, the cast came together again in a smash hit one-episode TV show, demonstrating how much they are loved by us.
Seeking to create a space to share the best moments and curiosities of the TV Show, this site was created and is open to everyone's contributions through the feedback  page.
